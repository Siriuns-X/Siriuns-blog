## Gamma function

$$
\Gamma(z) = \int_{0}^{+\infty} t^{z-1} e^{-t} \, \mathrm{d}t
$$

## Stokes' theorem

$$
\int_{\Omega} \mathrm{d}\omega = \int_{\partial\Omega} \omega
$$
